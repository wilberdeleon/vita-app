# Mission & Vision

**What is this?** Vita's mission, vision, and north star — the one-page answer to "why does this company exist?"

**Why does it exist?** Every feature, design, and business decision is tested against this page. If something conflicts with the mission, the mission wins.

*Source: verified from the repo Vision Lock (`docs/01-Vision-Lock.md`) and Product Bible (`docs/02-Product-Bible.md`).*

---

## Mission

**Build a healthier life, one decision at a time.**

Vita exists to help people become the healthiest version of themselves by transforming everyday health decisions into a meaningful, motivating, and measurable journey. Lasting transformation isn't created in a single day — it is built through consistent actions repeated over time. Vita helps people stay committed to that journey.

## Vision

**Become the world's most trusted personal health companion.**

Not simply an app that tracks calories. Not simply an app that tracks weight. A platform that helps people build healthier lives for years — a **premium transformation platform** combining health, nutrition, weight loss, longevity, coaching, biomarkers, habit building, and AI guidance.

## Core experience

**Transformation over tracking.** Most apps measure numbers; Vita measures transformation. Instead of only asking "What did you eat today?", Vita asks "**How are you changing?**" That shift defines everything we build.

## Primary emotion

> "I am becoming the person I want to become."

Every time someone opens Vita they should feel **motivated**, **in control**, and **proud of their progress**.

## The problem we solve

Most health apps collect information — calories, workouts, weight — but rarely help users feel connected to their progress. **People don't quit because they lack information. They quit because they lose motivation.** Vita exists to solve that.

## How we measure success

Not downloads. Success is people who keep opening Vita because it genuinely helps them build healthier lives.
