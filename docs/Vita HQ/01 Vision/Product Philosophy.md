# Product Philosophy

**What is this?** How Vita thinks about product — the pillars, the promise, the users, and the emotional contract with them.

**Why does it exist?** So every feature discussion starts from the same beliefs instead of re-deriving them.

*Source: verified from the repo Product Bible (`docs/02-Product-Bible.md`).*

---

## What Vita is — and is not

Vita is **not** a calorie tracker. Vita is **not** a workout tracker. Vita is a **premium transformation platform** whose purpose is helping people become the person they are capable of becoming.

Health is not a destination; it is a lifelong journey. Every healthy meal, every workout, every pound lost, every glass of water, every progress photo — every small decision matters. Vita makes those moments **visible and meaningful**.

## Our promise

Vita will never be built around guilt.

- We celebrate progress.
- We encourage consistency.
- We respect setbacks.
- Every day is another opportunity to continue the journey.

## The three pillars

| Pillar | Job | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Awareness** | Help users understand where they are today | weight, nutrition, photos, water, health metrics, habits |
| **Consistency** | Help users build habits through repetition | daily logging, weekly check-ins, milestones, reminders |
| **Transformation** | Help users see how they are changing over time | always visual, measurable, and personal |

## Our users

Anyone committed to improving their health: someone losing their first 20 pounds, training for a marathon, learning healthier habits, rebuilding confidence, using GLP-1 medications, tracking peptides, or beginning their very first health journey. **Everyone starts somewhere.**

## The VITA Promise — the feature test

Every feature must answer yes to all five:

1. Does this genuinely help someone improve their health?
2. Does this reinforce long-term consistency?
3. Does this reduce friction?
4. Does this help users understand their progress?
5. Would we proudly use this ourselves?

If the answer is no, it does not belong in Vita. No feature is built because a competitor has it.

## Brand personality

Professional. Encouraging. Intelligent. Supportive. **Never loud. Never gimmicky. Never overwhelming.** (Atlas embodies this — see [[Atlas Personality]].)
