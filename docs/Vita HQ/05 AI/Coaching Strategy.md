# Coaching Strategy

**What is this?** How Atlas coaches — the strategy layer between personality (who Atlas is) and capabilities (what Atlas can do).

**Why does it exist?** A coach with tools but no strategy is a chatbot with features. This page defines the coaching model before any AI ships, so capabilities get built in service of a method.

*Status: direction document. Nothing here is implemented ([[Atlas]] is a placeholder). Grounded in the verified Product Philosophy; the coaching model below is a working proposal for founder refinement.*

---

## The coaching thesis

People don't fail from lack of information — they fail from lack of motivation and accountability ([[Mission & Vision]]). Atlas therefore optimizes for **adherence, not knowledge transfer**: the best plan is the one the user actually follows.

## Working model (proposal — needs founder approval)

1. **Understand.** Atlas knows the user's goal, context, and history from their real data (Fuel logs, Journey trend, water, habits) — it never coaches blind.
2. **Strategize.** One clear priority at a time, aligned with "one decision at a time." Not ten tips — the *next* decision that matters most.
3. **Support.** Check-ins that reference reality ("three consistent weeks — your Momentum stage is earned") rather than generic cheerleading.
4. **Adapt.** Plans bend to real life. Missed days re-plan forward; they are never scolded backward ([[UX Principles]] #1).

## Accountability without guilt

The hard design problem. Direction: accountability = *noticing* + *re-inviting* ("Haven't seen a log since Tuesday — want to restart light today?"), never shaming, never streak-loss theater. Atlas frames every return as continuation, echoing the [[Journey Stages]] philosophy that progress is consistency, not perfection.

## Boundaries (safety)

- Atlas is a coach, **not a medical professional** — no diagnoses, no medication advice (relevant to [[Peptides]] data), clear escalation language for medical questions.
- Claims must be defensible — trust is the asset ([[Core Principles]] #5). This constrains [[Future Features|Health Age]] messaging too.

## Dependencies

- Real user data (Sprints 1–5) · model/provider architecture ([[Future AI]]) · founder approval of this model

## Open questions

- Proactive vs. reactive: does Atlas initiate (notifications) or wait to be opened? Proactive is more coach-like and more intrusive — founder taste decision.
- Frequency caps and quiet hours for coaching touches.
