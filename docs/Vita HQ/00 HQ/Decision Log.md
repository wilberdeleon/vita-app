# Decision Log

**What is this?** The permanent record of important product, design, and company decisions — what was decided, when, and where the decision is evidenced.

**Why does it exist?** So decisions are made once. When a debate resurfaces, this log answers "we already decided this, and here's why."

**How to use it:** newest entries first. Every entry names its **source** — `Repo` (verified in `vita-app` code/docs/git) or `Founders` (stated by the founders; not yet independently evidenced in the repo).
