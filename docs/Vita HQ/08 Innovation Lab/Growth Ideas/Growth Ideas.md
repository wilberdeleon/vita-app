# Growth Ideas

**Category of:** [[Innovation Lab]]

**Scope:** Acquisition, virality, referral, and retention mechanics — how Vita grows its user base and keeps people around, as distinct from what the product does for any single user.

**Examples of what fits:** referral programs, shareable transformation cards from [[My Journey]], founding-member cohorts, creator partnerships.

**Standing caution:** growth ideas answer to [[Core Principles]] #5 (trust) and #13 (health before growth) exactly like any feature — no dark patterns, no growth mechanic that undermines the no-guilt promise ([[UX Principles]]).
