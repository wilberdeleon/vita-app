# Experiments

**Category of:** [[Innovation Lab]]

**Scope:** Small, low-commitment tests meant to learn something — not full features. An experiment's point is the answer it produces, not the artifact itself. If it works, the *finding* graduates a different idea to 📋 Planned; the experiment note stays here as the record of what was learned.

**Examples of what fits:** A/B testing two onboarding flows, a one-week test of proactive vs. reactive Atlas nudges, a manual concierge test of meal planning before building automation.
