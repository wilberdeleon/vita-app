# Advanced Coaching (Proactive Check-Ins)

## Status

📝 Defined.

## Category

[[Atlas Features]]

## Priority

High. The specific capability that turns [[Coaching Strategy]]'s accountability thesis into something real.

## Estimated Difficulty

Medium. No open product questions blocking it (unlike [[Workout Generation]]) — mostly a notification-timing and copywriting problem once real user data exists.

## Recommended Tier

Atlas tier. *Recommendation only — see [[Business Model & Pricing]].*

## Problem

Users who go quiet don't need more information — they need to be noticed, gently, before quiet becomes gone. Reactive coaching (only responding when opened) misses the exact moment accountability matters most.

## Solution

Atlas proactively checks in based on real signals — a gap in logging, a stalled [[Journey Stages|Journey Stage]], a pattern shift — framed as noticing and re-inviting, never as guilt or streak-loss shaming ([[UX Principles]] #1).

## User Experience

After a defined quiet period, a notification arrives in Atlas's voice: "Haven't seen a log since Tuesday — want to restart light today?" Opening it lands in a short, warm re-entry conversation, not a dashboard of missed days. Frequency is capped and respects quiet hours (open question below) so it never feels naggy.

## Why Users Would Love It

It's accountability without shame — the exact thing most habit apps get wrong. A gentle nudge from a coach who "noticed" feels supportive; a red streak-broken banner feels punitive. This is the emotional core of what makes Atlas a coach and not a tracker.

## Business Value

Directly targets retention and win-back — the highest-leverage lifecycle moment in any habit product is the user about to churn. Reinforces the no-guilt brand promise in a way competitors' streak mechanics structurally cannot copy.

## Dependencies

- Real usage data to detect "gone quiet" signals (Sprints 1–5)
- Push notification infrastructure (not yet built)
- Copy design for sensitive moments — flagged as unresolved in [[Atlas Personality]]

## Future Enhancements

- Personalized quiet-period thresholds per user rather than one global rule
- Escalating warmth rather than escalating frequency for users who stay quiet longer
- Feeds into [[Voice Atlas]] once that capability exists
