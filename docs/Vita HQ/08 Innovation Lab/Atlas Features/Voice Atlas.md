# Voice Atlas

## Status

📋 Planned — promoted 2026-07-09. Named directly in the official [[Roadmap]] as Sprint 6 (Premium), Slice 5 — still deliberately the most distant capability, just now with a scheduled home rather than an open-ended "eventually."

## Category

[[Atlas Features]]

## Priority

Medium. High aspirational value, low near-term urgency — sequenced last on purpose ([[Atlas Capabilities]]).

## Estimated Difficulty

Very High. Combines unexplored voice infrastructure with the hardest version of the personality problem: cheesiness is far more fatal in voice than text.

## Recommended Tier

Elite — the most premium, most technically expensive capability in the Lab. *Recommendation only — see [[Business Model & Pricing]].*

## Problem

Typing to a coach has a ceiling — the moments people most want guidance (mid-walk, driving, cooking, at the gym) are exactly the moments typing is impractical. Text-only coaching misses Vita's most natural coaching moments.

## Solution

Conversational voice coaching with Atlas — talk to your coach the way you'd talk to a real one, in the same [[Atlas Personality]] register (calm, direct, professional) carried into speech.

## User Experience

A user opens a voice session with Atlas — hands-free, e.g. on a walk — and has a real spoken conversation: asking about today's plan, talking through a craving, getting a quick form check description. Atlas responds in a voice that matches its written personality: measured, confident, never chirpy. Session summaries land back in the app as text so nothing spoken is lost.

## Why Users Would Love It

It's the version of Atlas that feels most like an actual coach rather than an app feature — accessible in exactly the moments (walking, driving, working out) when typing isn't an option and motivation is highest.

## Business Value

The single most premium-feeling capability Vita could ship — a strong Elite-tier anchor and a genuine differentiator against every chat-only AI coach entering the market ([[Competitive Landscape]]).

## Dependencies

- **Text coaching must be proven first** — voice compounds whatever coaching quality already exists; building voice before the text foundation is solid means shipping a worse version twice
- Voice infrastructure entirely unexplored (provider, latency, on-device vs. cloud) — see [[Future AI]]
- Personality risk is highest here of any Atlas capability — needs dedicated tone work before launch ([[Atlas Personality]] open question on sensitive-moment copy)

## Future Enhancements

- Ambient/wearable-triggered check-ins once voice infra exists
- Multilingual support
