# Marketing & Growth

**What is this?** How people will find Vita and why they'll stay — positioning, messaging, and growth principles.

**Why does it exist?** So marketing grows from the product's actual identity instead of being bolted on at launch.

*Status: **no marketing work exists yet** (verified: nothing in the repo; no founder direction beyond the product identity itself). This page derives the foundations and marks the rest open.*

---

## Positioning (derived from verified product identity)

**Vita is the premium transformation platform** — for people ready to become who they're capable of becoming. Not a calorie tracker with a subscription; a health companion with a point of view.

## Messaging foundations (usable today)

- **The line that is the product:** *Build a healthier life, one decision at a time.*
- **The emotional promise:** "I am becoming the person I want to become."
- **The contrast story:** other apps ask what you ate; Vita asks how you're changing.
- **The anti-guilt stance:** the only health app that will never shame you for a missed day — genuinely rare, genuinely marketable.
- Tone follows the brand personality ([[Product Philosophy]]): professional, encouraging, never loud. Marketing that sounds like a gym promo has failed before it runs.

## Growth principles (constraints, decided by the product's laws)

1. **Retention before acquisition.** Success is people who keep opening Vita ([[Mission & Vision]]) — pouring users into an unproven bucket violates "think long-term."
2. **Trust-compatible growth only.** No dark patterns, no data-driven ad targeting off health data ([[Core Principles]] #5).
3. **The product is the funnel.** Transformation stories ([[My Journey]] progress, [[Journey Stages]]) are inherently shareable — with user consent, this is the organic engine.
