# Launch Plan

**What is this?** The path from today's founder-tested build to a public App Store launch.

**Why does it exist?** Launch readiness is more than finished sprints — accounts, compliance, and positioning all have lead times. This page tracks the full checklist.

*Status: **no launch date or plan has been set** (verified: nothing in the repo beyond the Version 1 definition). This is the working skeleton for founders to shape.*

---

## Launch gate: Version 1 (per the official roadmap, 2026-07-09)

Every primary screen implemented · complete navigation · visual consistency · Product Bible + Design System + Build Handbook compliance · Atlas functioning as a real AI coach. **Sprint 7 — Beta** in the [[Roadmap]] is the final gate — its own deliverable ("a stable, production-ready beta") and slice list (Bug Fixes, Performance, Accessibility, Offline, Analytics, Crash Reporting, App Store Preparation, Final QA & Launch Checklist) overlaps directly with the pre-launch checklist below; worth reconciling the two next time either page is touched.

## Working phases (proposal)

1. **Founder alpha (now).** Expo Go on founders' iPhones; mock data ([[Deployment & Builds]]).
2. **Real-data alpha.** Supabase live, real auth, founders using Vita daily for their own health — the "would we use it every day?" principle, tested literally.
3. **Private beta.** TestFlight, small invited group; retention (do people keep opening it?) is the metric, per [[Mission & Vision]].
4. **App Store launch.** V1 complete, polished, premium.
