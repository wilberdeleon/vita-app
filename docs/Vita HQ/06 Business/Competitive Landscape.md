# Competitive Landscape

**What is this?** Who Vita competes with, and — more importantly — how Vita is positioned against them.

**Why does it exist?** Not to copy anyone ("no feature is built because another app has it" — [[Product Philosophy]]), but to keep Vita's differentiation sharp and honest.

*Status: positioning below follows from verified product documents; the competitor read is general market knowledge as of mid-2026 — refresh with real research before [[Launch Plan|launch]] marketing. No competitor analysis exists in the repo.*

---

## The landscape Vita enters

| Category | Examples | What they are | What they miss (Vita's read) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Calorie trackers | MyFitnessPal, Lose It, Cronometer | Databases with logging | Information without motivation — the exact failure Vita exists to solve |
| Wearable platforms | **Oura, WHOOP** | Premium recovery/readiness insight | Hardware-gated; measure state, don't coach transformation |
| Platform hubs | Apple Health/Fitness+ | Data aggregation + workouts | A hub, not a companion; no journey narrative |
| GLP-1 era apps | weight-loss/med-adjacent apps | Medication-centric weight loss | Rarely built as long-term premium companions; Vita serves these users ([[Peptides]]) without being defined by them |
| AI coach apps | growing wave of ChatGPT-wrapper coaches | Chat about health | No real user data underneath — exactly the trap [[Future AI]] insists Vita avoid |

## Vita's position

**The premium transformation companion — WHOOP/Oura-grade experience, no hardware required, measuring who you're becoming rather than what you did.**

Differentiators, from the verified product docs:
1. **Transformation over tracking** — [[Journey Stages]] as narrative, not streaks
2. **No guilt, structurally** — a product law competitors' engagement mechanics can't copy without breaking themselves
3. **Premium design** at a bar fitness apps don't attempt ([[Design Bible]])
4. **Atlas** — coaching grounded in the user's own data ([[Coaching Strategy]])
5. **Longevity trajectory** — [[Future Features|Health Age / Biomarker Age]] as the long game

## Standing rule

Competitor features enter the [[Innovation Lab]] like any idea and pass the VITA Promise or die. Watch competitors for *positioning*, not roadmaps.
