# Idea Inbox

**What is this?** The capture point for raw ideas — features, design notions, business thoughts — before they earn a place anywhere else.

**Why does it exist?** Ideas are cheap to capture and expensive to lose. Parking them here keeps the Roadmap disciplined: nothing enters the roadmap without passing the VITA Promise and the "Build for the Next 10 Years" test.

> The repo also has `docs/10-Ideas-Parking-Lot.md` (currently empty). Engineering-adjacent ideas that affect the codebase should graduate there; HQ is the wider funnel for product/design/business ideas.

## How ideas graduate

1. Captured here with a date and one honest sentence.
2. Discussed — does it pass the VITA Promise? Would we use it every day?
3. If yes → promoted to Future Features (product) or the Roadmap (committed), and logged in the Decision Log if it changes direction.
4. If no → marked **Declined** with the reason. Declined ideas stay visible; they prevent re-litigating.
